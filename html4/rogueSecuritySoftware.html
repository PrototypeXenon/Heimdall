Rogue Security Software

These will be popups warning you of malicious software where, when clicked and opened, give you malicious software.

Popups warning you of malicious software on your computer, can actually be software that downloads malicious onto your computer. These popups appear legitimate and ask you to click on a link to install the "update", or "remove" unwanted malicious software that they may have detected. This could be rogue security software, designed to lure people into clicking and downloading malicious software. It may appear as a pop up that states your computer is at risk, or may already have a virus, and asks you to click a link to allow it to "fix" the problem. In all reality, you are just letting them access your computer remotely, and the software has a better chance of stealing your personal information.
