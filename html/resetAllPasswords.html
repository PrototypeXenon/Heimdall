Reset All Passwords

Make sure to reset your passwords if you feel as though there has been any suspicious activity on any of your accounts.

Before getting deleted, malware could have captured passwords and revealed them to others. Change each and every password you can think of, so that there is a smaller chance of getting hacked. It is recommended that passwords should be at least eight characters with at least one number, and possibly even a symbol, if it is possible. Doing this may help prevent from future attacks and hacks on your account(s).
