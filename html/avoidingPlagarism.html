Avoiding Plagarism

Make sure to give credit to the owner and cite the work. Make sure you get permission to use someones music to avoid copyright infragments. It's always a good idea to check with whoever you're submitting your project to in order to determine whether or not you can use your sources.

To avoid plagiarism, you should get permission from the person who created the work. You can change the wording to make your own thoughts, but you should still credit the original creator. A works cited or resouces page should be easy to find. It's a good idea to read up on Creative Commons and Copyright laws for the specific license for the material you're using.
