Ads For Free Stuff

These will advertise free stuff, but in reality just give you viruses

If you ever get a pop up that says "YOU WIN" or "CLICK HERE FOR FREE MINECRAFT" the majority of the time this is a tactic to get private information out of you, or to get bugs onto your computer. Do not trust these sites or advertisements. If you are on a website that is asking for things that don't make sense for the website to need, like your credit card number for online surveys, ...This is a method for hackers to get your personal information.
