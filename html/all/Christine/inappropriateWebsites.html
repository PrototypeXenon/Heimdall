Inappropriate Websites

Sexual or graphic content can put viruses on your computer and you will not know they are there until those viruses infect your computer and start to ruin your files or ask for ransom.

If you stumble upon a website containing sexual or graphic content exit the page, if you return to the website many of times you are putting yourself, your family, and your computer at risk of malware. Your family (mainly kids) are at risk of seeing some age restriced content or sesitive content. After being on the website check computer for viruses and malware.
