Computer Worm

These are a more complex versions of viruses that travel faster and more efficiently through the compter. They cause the system to slow down or crash unexpectedly without warning.

A computer worm is a software program that can copy itself from one computer to another, without human interaction. Worms can replicate in great volume and with great speed. These can attack files and your computer and can do great harm to the computer software/hardware also can copy to other services and go to another computer. They can also expose private information to the entire world without human interaction or just be annoying like putting random sounds, pictures, and messages.
